This Privacy Policy explains how Dairy B2B Orders, developed by Syslinker, collects, uses, and protects your information when using our mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, address, and profile details.

  • Permissions: Camera or gallery access (only when uploading images).

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account

  • To improve app performance and fix issues
  • To ensure security and prevent fraud

Data Sharing

Dairy B2B Orders does not sell your information to anyone. We only share data with:

Data Security

We use industry-standard security through Firebase to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure, but we follow best practices.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children below 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
